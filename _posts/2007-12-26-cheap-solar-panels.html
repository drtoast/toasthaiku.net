--- layout: post title: Cheap Solar Panels wordpress_id: 42 date: 2007-12-26 15:07:24.000000000 -08:00 tags: - energy - solar --- Nanosolar is now producing panels that make solar power cheaper than coal for the first time.  Current photovoltaic panels use expensive and fragile silicon wafers.  Nanosolar has developed a process to "print" photovoltaic material onto flexible rolls of aluminum cheaply and quickly, producing solar panels for less than $1 a watt, and enabling construction of large solar power plants for $2 a watt (coal plants cost about $2.10 a watt to build).  The cost should continue to drop as production scales up.
