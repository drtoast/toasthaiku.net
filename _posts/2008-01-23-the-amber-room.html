--- layout: post title: The Amber Room wordpress_id: 64 date: 2008-01-23 15:30:10.000000000 -08:00 tags: - art - history --- In 1716, the King of Prussia gave Russian czar Peter the Great a gift of a 180-square foot room encrusted with six tons of amber panels backed with gold leaf, aptly known as The Amber Room. Invading Nazis looted the room in 1941, packing the amber into crates and hightailing it back to Germany. The missing panels have never been found.
